Small teams do not need a giant AI transformation program. They need a clear starting point.

The best first AI projects are narrow, visible, and tied to work people already understand. This could mean a research assistant, a proposal drafting flow, a support knowledge base, or an internal policy guide.

## Start with one useful loop

Pick one repeated task. Map the inputs, the decision points, the human review step, and the output. Then design AI support around that loop.

## Write the rules early

Responsible AI becomes easier when expectations are written down before the tool spreads. Define what data is allowed, where review is required, and who owns quality.

That is the golden standard: not more AI everywhere, but better AI where it matters.
