Automation should never feel like a black box taking over the room. The strongest AI workflows show their reasoning boundaries, keep decisions reviewable, and make it easy for people to correct course.

## Make the handoff visible

People need to know when a system is drafting, recommending, deciding, or acting. Each mode has a different level of risk and should be designed with clear controls.

## Keep reversibility close

Trust grows when users can inspect what happened and undo actions. This matters even more in customer support, operations, research, and knowledge management.

Human-ready automation is not slower. It is calmer, clearer, and more resilient.
